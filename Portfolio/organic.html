DIY Organic Fertilizer Recipes

  • Banana Peel Fertilizer: Chop banana peels and bury them in the soil for potassium.
  • Eggshell Fertilizer: Crush eggshells and mix them into the soil for calcium.
  • Compost Tea: Steep compost in water for a nutrient-rich liquid fertilizer.

Benefits of Using Organic Fertilizers

  • Improves soil health and structure.
  • Enhances microbial activity in the soil.
  • Reduces the risk of chemical runoff.
  • Promotes sustainable gardening practices.

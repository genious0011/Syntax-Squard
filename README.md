## 📌 Overview

Food wastage is one of the most pressing issues facing our planet today. Every year, billions of tons of food go to waste—while millions of people remain hungry. This project aims to raise awareness about the environmental, economic, and social impacts of food wastage and explores creative solutions to minimize it.
